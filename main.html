How to Play


Welcome to "Guess My Number!"


Here are the rules:


  1. Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.

  2. Guess the secret number within the range specified by the difficulty level.

  3. You have a limited number of attempts based on the difficulty level.

  4. If your guess is too high or too low, you will receive a hint.

  5. Try to guess the number before you run out of attempts!


Good luck and have fun!
