How to Play


Welcome to "Guess My Number!"


Here are the rules:


  1. Select a difficulty level: Easy, Medium or Hard.

  2. Guess the secret number within the range specified based on the difficulty level.

  3. You will recieve limited number of attempts based on the difficulty level you choose.

  4. If your guess is high or low, you will receive a hint.

  5. Try to guess the number before you run out of attempts!

  6. On each correct guess you will earn 5 points as score

Good luck and have fun!
